# Module 1 summary

- **AI** is the broad field; **ML** learns from data; **deep learning** uses deep neural nets; **generative AI** creates new content.
- Today's products are almost always **narrow AI**, not AGI.
- The **AI effect** means successful techniques fade into "just software."
- Use the **glossary** when terms reappear later.
