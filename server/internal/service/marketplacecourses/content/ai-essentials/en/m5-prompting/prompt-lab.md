# Prompt Lab

**Goal:** Practice iterating a prompt and verifying the output (learning outcomes 3 and 4).

## Instructions

1. Pick a **real, low-stakes task** you might do at work or school (for example: outline a meeting agenda, rewrite a paragraph more clearly, or brainstorm study questions). Do **not** paste personal, confidential, or proprietary data into a third-party AI tool.
2. Write an **initial prompt**.
3. **Iterate at least once** (change goal, constraints, or format).
4. Submit a short reflection that includes all of the following:

### Required submission structure

- **Task:** one sentence describing what you tried to accomplish
- **Prompt A:** your initial prompt (paste text)
- **Prompt B:** your improved prompt (paste text)
- **What changed:** 2–4 sentences on what you changed and why
- **Evaluation:** 3–5 sentences on the output's accuracy/usefulness and **one thing you verified** against a non-AI source or your own knowledge

## Rubric (full credit for a good-faith, on-rubric submission)

| Criterion | What full credit looks like |
|---|---|
| Iteration | Two distinct prompts; clear note on what changed |
| Verification | Names at least one concrete verification step |
| Privacy | No sensitive personal/confidential data pasted |
| Completeness | All required sections present |

This assignment is auto-graded (`grader_agent`). You receive full credit for a complete, good-faith submission meeting the rubric. Optional AI feedback may appear when enabled on the platform.

**Privacy reminder:** do not paste secrets, passwords, health details, or others' private data into external tools.
