# Capstone: Responsible AI Use-Case Analysis

**Goal:** Demonstrate outcomes 3 and 5 — judge fit and reason about responsibility.

## Scenario

Choose a realistic scenario (you may use this default): **"Use AI to draft customer support emails."** Or pick another workplace/school scenario of similar scope.

## Write (submit as text)

Address all three parts:

**(a) Fit:** Is AI appropriate for this task? Why or why not? Name at least one limitation that matters here (for example hallucination, bias, privacy, or lack of ground-truth understanding).

**(b) Trustworthiness:** Name **one** NIST AI RMF trustworthiness characteristic that is at risk in your scenario (valid & reliable; safe; secure & resilient; accountable & transparent; explainable & interpretable; privacy-enhanced; fair with harmful bias managed). Explain briefly why.

**(c) Mitigation:** Describe **one concrete mitigation** and the **human-oversight** step (who reviews what before the output is used).

## Length

About 200–400 words total is enough. Quality of reasoning matters more than length.

| (c) | Concrete mitigation + explicit human-oversight step |
