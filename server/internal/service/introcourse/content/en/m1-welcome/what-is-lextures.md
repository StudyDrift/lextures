## Welcome

Lextures is a learning platform that helps you find your courses, complete activities, and track progress in one place — on the web or on your phone.

### What you will learn here

This short course walks through the features you will use most often: navigating courses, submitting work, understanding how Lextures adapts to you, and where to get help.

It takes about **30–45 minutes** if you read each page and try the linked activities.

### Who this is for

Whether you are in a classroom, studying on your own, or evaluating Lextures for your organization, the same tour applies. Optional sections appear only when your institution has those features turned on.

[Open your course list](/courses) when you are ready to explore alongside this tour.
