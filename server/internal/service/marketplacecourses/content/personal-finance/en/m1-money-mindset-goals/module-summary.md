# Module 1 summary

- Connect money choices to **values**, not only to rules of thumb.
- Label **needs vs. wants** so spending matches priorities.
- Write **SMART** goals with clear amounts and timelines (illustrative figures are fine).
- Use **MyMoney Five** (Earn, Save & Invest, Protect, Spend, Borrow) as a map for lifelong money skills.
- Keep the **glossary** handy for later modules.

Next: turn goals into a monthly budget and cash-flow plan.
