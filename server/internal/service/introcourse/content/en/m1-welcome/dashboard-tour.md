## Home and courses

After you sign in, your **Courses** page is home base. Each course card opens that course's modules, assignments, grades, and announcements.

### Try it

[Open your dashboard](/courses) and locate this intro course — **Welcome to Lextures**.

### What you will see on a course card

- Course title and instructor
- Upcoming due dates (when configured)
- A quick way to resume where you left off

### Settings and account

Open **Settings** from the navigation menu to update your profile, notification preferences, and integrations.

[Open account settings](/settings/account)
