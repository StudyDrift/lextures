# Module 5 summary

- Risk and return trade off; **diversification** spreads risk.
- Understand **index funds**, **fees**, and **tax-advantaged** retirement accounts — verify current limits at [IRS.gov](https://www.irs.gov/).
- Spot **fraud red flags** like guaranteed high returns with little risk.
- Complete the **Compounding Exercise** with the Investor.gov calculator.
