# The MyMoney Five framework

> **Educational disclaimer:** This course is for **education only**. It is **US-focused** and is **not** personalized financial, tax, investment, or legal advice. Rules and figures change — verify current details at official sources linked in each lesson. For decisions about your situation, consider a qualified professional.

[MyMoney Five](https://www.mymoney.gov/mymoney-five-tools) is a U.S. federal framework that groups money skills into five themes:

1. **Earn** — income, benefits, and growing earning power
2. **Save & Invest** — building reserves and longer-term growth
3. **Protect** — insurance, fraud awareness, and safeguarding assets
4. **Spend** — budgeting and living within your means
5. **Borrow** — using credit carefully and managing debt

## How this course maps

| MyMoney Five | Where we cover it |
|---|---|
| Earn / Spend | Modules 1–2 (goals, budgeting) |
| Save & Invest | Modules 3 and 5 |
| Borrow | Module 4 |
| Protect | Modules 6–7 (insurance, scams) |

You do not need to master every theme at once. Use the framework as a map: when a tip online only talks about "hustle" or "hot stocks," ask which MyMoney Five area it actually helps — and what it ignores.
