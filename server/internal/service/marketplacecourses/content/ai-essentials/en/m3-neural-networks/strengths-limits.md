# Strengths, limits, and honest expectations

Deep models can look fluent and confident while being wrong. Fluency is not understanding.

## Strengths

- Scale to messy real-world inputs
- Improve with more relevant data (up to a point)
- Transfer learning: reuse representations across related tasks

## Limits

- Need data that matches the deployment setting
- Can amplify bias present in training data
- Hard to fully explain every decision in large models
- Costly to train from scratch (most learners use pre-trained systems)
