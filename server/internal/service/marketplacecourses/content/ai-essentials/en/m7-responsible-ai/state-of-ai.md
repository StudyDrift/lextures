# Further reading / state of AI

This page isolates **dated** claims so the rest of the course stays evergreen. Refresh it when you re-provision content.

## Where to look for current statistics

- [Stanford HAI AI Index](https://hai.stanford.edu/ai-index) — updated annually with research, investment, and public-attitude statistics
- [NIST AI RMF](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) — risk-management guidance (framework text is stable; companion resources evolve)

## How to read headlines

- Prefer primary reports over viral posts
- Note the **as-of** date on any benchmark
- Ask whether a claim is about a lab demo, a narrow product, or AGI speculation
