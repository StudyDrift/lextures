## Why Lextures nudges what it nudges

Lextures is built around a few learning science ideas: practice strengthens memory, feedback helps you adjust, and spaced review keeps knowledge fresh.

This module explains the **patterns** you may notice — recommendations, review prompts, and diagnostics — and why they exist to help you learn, not to judge you.
