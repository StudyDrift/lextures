# Images, audio, and multimodal generation

Generative models also create images, audio, and video, or combine modalities (text → image). The same literacy rules apply: outputs reflect training patterns; they can be biased, wrong, or misleading; and high-stakes use needs review.

Vendor tools change quickly. Prefer **vendor-neutral** skills: clear goals, iteration, verification, and privacy hygiene — taught next — over memorizing one product's UI.
