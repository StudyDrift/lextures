# Module 3 summary

- Neural nets stack simple units into **layers**; "deep" means many layers.
- Progress came from **data + compute + methods**, not magic.
- Deep learning shines at pattern-heavy tasks and struggles where truth, fairness, or causality must be guaranteed.

Next: generative AI and large language models.
