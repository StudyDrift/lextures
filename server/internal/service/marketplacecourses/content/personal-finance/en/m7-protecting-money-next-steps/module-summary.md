# Module 7 summary

- Recognize **scam** patterns and **identity theft** risks; pause and verify via official channels.
- Prefer **.gov / nonprofit** education over sales funnels.
- Keep a **simple written plan** and update it when life changes.
- Submit the **capstone** with illustrative figures only.

You now have a foundation to budget, save, manage credit, understand investing basics, plan for big costs, and protect yourself — and a map of trustworthy places to learn more.
