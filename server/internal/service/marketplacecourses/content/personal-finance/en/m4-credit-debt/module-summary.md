# Module 4 summary

- Review **credit reports** via [AnnualCreditReport.com](https://www.annualcreditreport.com/) and understand what **scores** summarize.
- Compare borrowing costs with **APR**; use credit carefully.
- **Avalanche** targets highest interest first; **snowball** targets smallest balance first.
- Complete the **Debt-Payoff Plan** with illustrative debts only.
