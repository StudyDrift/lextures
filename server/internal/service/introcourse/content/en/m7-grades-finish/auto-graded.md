## Auto-graded activities

**Auto-graded** means Lextures scores your work automatically — common for multiple-choice quizzes and some structured responses.

Essays, file uploads, and open-ended work usually need instructor review. The activity will show **submitted** until a grade is posted.

Knowledge checks in this intro course are auto-graded so you can see instant feedback.
