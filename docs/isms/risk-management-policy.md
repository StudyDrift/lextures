# Risk Management Policy

**ISO clauses:** 6.1, 8.2; Annex A.5.7 (threat intelligence)

## Process

1. **Identify** information security risks to confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the LMS and customer data.
2. **Assess** likelihood (1–5) and impact (1–5); residual score = likelihood × impact.
3. **Treat** via mitigate, accept, transfer, or avoid.
4. **Review** annually or when significant changes occur.

## Risk register

Active risks are stored in `compliance.risk_register` and managed through the compliance admin API.

**Acceptance:** Residual risks with score ≥ 15 require CTO sign-off before acceptance.
