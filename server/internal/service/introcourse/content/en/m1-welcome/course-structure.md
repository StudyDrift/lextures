## Inside a course

Courses are organized into **modules** — ordered sections that group related lessons and activities.

### Common item types

| Type | What it is |
|------|------------|
| **Content page** | Reading with text, images, and links (like this page) |
| **Assignment** | Work you submit for instructor feedback |
| **Quiz** | Auto-scored knowledge checks |
| **External link** | A shortcut to a resource outside the course |

### Navigation tips

- Use the course sidebar to jump between modules.
- Your progress is saved as you go — you can leave and return anytime.
- Due dates appear on the course calendar when instructors set them.

[View your calendar](/calendar) to see upcoming dates across courses.
