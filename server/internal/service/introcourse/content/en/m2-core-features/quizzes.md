## Quizzes

**Quizzes** check your understanding. Many are auto-graded; some include essay or file-upload questions that need instructor review.

- Select **Submit** when you are finished — not only when a timer ends.
- Review rules vary: you may see correct answers immediately, after the due date, or never, depending on course settings.
- Time limits and question shuffling are shown before you start.
