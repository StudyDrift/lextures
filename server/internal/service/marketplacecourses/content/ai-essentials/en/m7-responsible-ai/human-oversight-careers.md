# Human oversight and AI in everyday work

**Human oversight** means people review, approve, or can intervene — especially when rights, safety, money, or reputation are on the line.

## Where AI already shows up

- Recommendations and search ranking
- Writing and coding assistants
- Fraud alerts and spam filters
- Accessibility tools (captions, transcription)

## How work is changing

Tasks shift toward specifying goals, verifying outputs, and integrating AI into workflows — not toward "never think again." Skills from this course (fit judgment, prompting, verification, responsibility) transfer across tools.

See [AI for Everyone](https://www.deeplearning.ai/courses/ai-for-everyone) for a non-technical view of AI in organizations.
