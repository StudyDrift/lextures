# Module 2 summary

- A **budget** plans income and expenses; **cash flow** shows what is left.
- **50/30/20** is a flexible guideline; **zero-based** assigns every dollar a job.
- **Track** spending to find leaks; build an **emergency fund** over time.
- Complete the **Monthly Budget** assignment with illustrative figures only.
