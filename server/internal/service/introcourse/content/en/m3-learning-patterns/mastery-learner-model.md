## Mastery and the learner model

As you answer questions and complete activities, Lextures builds a **learner model** — an estimate of what you have mastered and what needs more practice.

You do not manage this model directly. It updates from your real work so recommendations stay relevant.

> **AI note:** When AI-assisted features are enabled at your institution, adaptive recommendations may use automated analysis of your learning activity. See your institution's AI disclosure settings for details.
