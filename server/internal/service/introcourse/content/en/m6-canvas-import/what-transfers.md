## What comes across

Depending on your import options, Lextures can transfer:

- **Modules** and content pages
- **Assignments** and **quizzes**
- **Rubrics** and point values
- **Announcements**
- Prior **submissions and grades** (when selected)

Some Canvas-specific tools may map to Lextures equivalents or arrive as external links. Review the import summary before publishing to students.
