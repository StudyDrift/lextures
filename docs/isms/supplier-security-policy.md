# Supplier / Sub-processor Security Policy

**ISO control:** A.5.19–A.5.22 (supplier relationships)

## Requirements (AC-5)

Before a sub-processor handles production customer data:

1. Complete a security questionnaire **or** obtain a current ISO 27001 or SOC 2 Type II report (< 12 months).
2. Record the review in `compliance.supplier_reviews`.
3. Publish the sub-processor on the [Trust Center](/trust) sub-processor list (GDPR Art. 28 / FERPA).

## Review cadence

Critical-tier vendors: annual review. All others: at contract renewal or material change.
