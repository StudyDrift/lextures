## Your grades

Open **Grades** inside a course to see released scores and feedback. Some items stay hidden until the instructor posts grades for everyone.

Instructors use the gradebook to record scores, excused statuses, and comments. You see only what they release to students.

[Open this course's gradebook](/courses/C-WLCOME/gradebook) to see how grades appear.
