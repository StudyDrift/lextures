## Your learner profile

The **learner profile** is an autonomous summary of how you learn on Lextures — derived from your activity across courses, not typed in by you or an instructor.

It powers adaptive recommendations and helps you understand *why* the platform suggests certain next steps.

### Privacy

Lextures does not sell your profile. For how your data is used, see the [Privacy Center](/privacy-centre).
