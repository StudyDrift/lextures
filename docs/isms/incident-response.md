# Information Security Incident Response

**ISO controls:** A.5.24–A.5.28

Lextures follows the incident response procedure documented on the Trust Center (Security Overview → Incident Response). Incidents affecting customer data are:

1. Logged in `compliance.incidents` (SOC 2 plan 10.9 — shared evidence)
2. Triaged within 1 hour
3. Root-caused within 24 hours
4. Customer notification within 72 hours where GDPR Art. 33 applies

Post-incident corrective actions are tracked in `compliance.iso_audit_findings` when they arise from internal or certification audits.
