# Tax basics and withholding

> **Educational disclaimer:** This course is for **education only**. It is **US-focused** and is **not** personalized financial, tax, investment, or legal advice. Rules and figures change — verify current details at official sources linked in each lesson. For decisions about your situation, consider a qualified professional.

This module is a **map**, not tax preparation. U.S. federal tax rules and figures change — always verify at [IRS.gov](https://www.irs.gov/).

## Core ideas

- **Income tax** is based on taxable income after allowed adjustments/deductions (details vary).
- **Withholding** is money held from paychecks toward taxes. Form W-4 (employee withholding) settings affect how much is withheld.
- Many people file an annual return to reconcile what was withheld vs. what is owed.

## Where to file / get forms

Use official IRS channels for forms, filing options, and current-year information: [IRS.gov](https://www.irs.gov/). Beware look-alike sites.

## What this course will not do

- Prepare your return
- Tell you which credits to claim
- Hard-code tax brackets or standard deduction amounts that go stale

For consumer money context around income and benefits, see also [CFPB](https://www.consumerfinance.gov/) and [MyMoney.gov](https://www.mymoney.gov/).
