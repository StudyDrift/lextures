## Mobile apps

Lextures offers **iOS and Android** apps that use the same account as the web. Search your device's app store for **Lextures**, install, and sign in with the same email and password (or SSO) you use here.

After sign-in you will see the same courses and can complete many activities on the go.

[Review your account settings](/settings/account) for profile and security options that apply on every device.
