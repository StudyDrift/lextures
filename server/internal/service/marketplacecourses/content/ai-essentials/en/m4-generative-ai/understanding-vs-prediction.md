# "Understanding" vs. prediction

LLMs can summarize, translate, and brainstorm because those tasks align with patterns in text. That can look like understanding.

## What they do well

- Drafting and revising prose
- Explaining concepts at a chosen level (with verification)
- Transforming formats (bullets ↔ paragraphs)

## What they do *not* reliably do

- Guarantee factual correctness
- Know your private documents unless you provide them (and you should be careful doing so)
- Replace domain experts for high-stakes decisions
- Possess grounded, real-world experience

When content in this course shows example AI output, treat it as **illustrative** — it may be wrong.

[Generative AI for Everyone](https://www.coursera.org/learn/generative-ai-for-everyone) focuses on practical use without coding.
