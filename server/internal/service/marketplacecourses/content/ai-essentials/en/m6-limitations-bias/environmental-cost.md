# Environmental and cost notes

Training and running large models consumes energy and money. Exact figures change quickly and depend on hardware, electricity mix, and usage patterns — so this course does **not** treat any single vendor benchmark as timeless fact.

Practical takeaway: use the smallest tool that meets the need; avoid unnecessary huge-context calls; and prefer organizational guidance on approved tools.

For periodically updated industry statistics, see the [Stanford HAI AI Index](https://hai.stanford.edu/ai-index) (refresh the Module 7 "state of AI" page when numbers age).
