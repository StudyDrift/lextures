## Facets and provenance

Each **facet** of your profile describes one dimension — for example, preferred content modalities, strengths, or study patterns.

Every insight shows **provenance**: what activity contributed (quiz attempts, page views, practice sessions) and when it was last updated. You can expand evidence to see counts and time windows.

The profile builds over time. Early on you may see a "still building" state — keep learning and it will fill in.
