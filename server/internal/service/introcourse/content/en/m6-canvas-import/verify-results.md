## After import

Watch the **progress log** while the import runs. When it finishes:

- Open each imported course and spot-check modules and pages
- Confirm assignments and quizzes open correctly
- Compare a sample grade or submission if you imported student data
- Publish the course when you are satisfied

Keep your Canvas course available until you have verified everything important transferred.
