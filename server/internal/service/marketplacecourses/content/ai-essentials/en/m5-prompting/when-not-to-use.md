# When not to use AI

AI tools are a poor fit when:

- You need **guaranteed** correctness (legal filings, medical decisions, safety-critical controls) without expert review
- You would have to paste **sensitive** personal or confidential data into a third-party tool
- The task requires **your** authentic voice or assessment (some academic integrity policies)
- You cannot verify the output and the cost of being wrong is high

When in doubt: use AI for brainstorming or drafting, then verify with humans and primary sources. See also [AI for Everyone](https://www.deeplearning.ai/courses/ai-for-everyone).
