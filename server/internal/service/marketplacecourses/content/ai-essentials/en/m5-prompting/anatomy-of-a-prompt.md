# Anatomy of a good prompt

A **prompt** is the instruction you give a generative model. Strong prompts are specific about goal, audience, constraints, and format.

## Building blocks

1. **Role or context** (optional): "You are helping a project manager…"
2. **Task**: what to produce
3. **Constraints**: length, tone, must-include / must-avoid
4. **Format**: bullets, table, email draft
5. **Inputs**: the raw material the model should use

## Weak vs. strong

- Weak: "Write about marketing."
- Stronger: "Draft a 150-word email to existing customers announcing a 10% loyalty discount next Friday. Friendly tone. Do not invent product features. End with one clear call to action."

[Generative AI for Everyone](https://www.coursera.org/learn/generative-ai-for-everyone) and [AI for Everyone](https://www.deeplearning.ai/courses/ai-for-everyone) both emphasize clear goals over clever tricks.
