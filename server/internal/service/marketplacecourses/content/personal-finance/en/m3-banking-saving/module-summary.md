# Module 3 summary

- Use **checking** for spending and **savings** for reserves; compare fees and access.
- Prefer **APY** when comparing deposit yields; **compounding** grows balances over time.
- **FDIC insurance** protects covered deposits at insured banks up to standard limits — verify current rules at official sources.
- Practice compounding with illustrative numbers on Investor.gov (Module 5 assignment).
